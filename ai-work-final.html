Understanding the Shift

What AI Actually Does
to Your Work

AI doesn't solve problems ‚Äî it executes solutions. The irreplaceable part of any role is the judgment, creativity, and domain knowledge that precedes execution.

The Execution Layer is Gone. What Remains is Everything Else.

Think of every professional's workflow as two phases: the thinking phase ‚Äî where you understand a problem, devise strategy, make trade-offs ‚Äî and the execution phase ‚Äî where you write, code, calculate, or produce output. AI has absorbed the execution phase entirely. A developer no longer spends 3 hours writing boilerplate once the architecture is clear ‚Äî Copilot does it in seconds. A financial analyst no longer manually builds models ‚Äî GPT-4 generates the spreadsheet. What remains entirely human? Knowing what problem to solve, how to frame it, and whether the solution makes sense.
